Questions

Frequently asked

What exactly is WriteandBill?

An all‑in‑one EHR, medical billing and practice management platform. You document care naturally; the platform structures the record, suggests codes with evidence, scrubs and submits claims, captures quality data, and gives you clean reports — all in one system designed by medical professionals.

Will AI write notes for me?

Yes — via dictation, ambient capture or expanding your shorthand. You always review and sign. The AI drafts and organizes; the clinician stays the author of record.

Can it really be customized for my practice?

Yes. Note styles, specialty content, schedules, report templates, task routing and permissions are configurable per practice, per location and per provider — without consultants or long "build" projects.

Does it integrate with our existing tools?

WriteandBill ships with an open REST API, API‑key management, webhooks and FHIR export, so labs, clearinghouses, HIEs and analytics tools connect without interface ransom fees.

How does it help with MIPS and regulatory requirements?

Quality measures are mapped to your normal documentation, so the data is captured as a by‑product of care. Dashboards show your standing continuously, and audit‑ready logs are always available.

Who is behind WriteandBill?

A team of practicing clinicians and health‑IT veterans with decades of experience inside legacy digital healthcare systems — and in the federal, state‑specific and payer‑specific legal and regulatory requirements that burden practices. That first‑hand experience with both the software and the rules is exactly why WriteandBill exists.

Is our data secure?

Role‑based access, full audit logging, and encryption in transit and at rest are foundational. The platform is engineered for HIPAA‑aligned operations from day one.
